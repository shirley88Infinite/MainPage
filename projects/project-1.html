Inspiration

In 2024, I came across numerous reports and videos suggesting possible signs of life on Mars, along with intriguing UFO and UAP footage. These ideas sparked my imagination and inspired me to create a game that blends cosmic mystery with a futuristic setting. The result is a concept rooted in a modern cyberpunk city, where players pilot a spacecraft to embark on daring interstellar adventures.
