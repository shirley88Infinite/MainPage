Inspiration

In 2024, I came across numerous reports and videos suggesting possible signs of life on Mars, along with intriguing UFO and UAP footage. These ideas sparked my imagination and inspired me to create a game that blends cosmic mystery with a futuristic setting. The result is a concept rooted in a modern cyberpunk city, where players pilot a spacecraft to embark on daring interstellar adventures.

Overview

Interstellar 2025 is a simple RPG-style game where players pilot a spaceship to explore the scene and navigate through the environment. The focus is on free movement, discovery, and building the foundation for future gameplay expansion.

Features

  • Audio Settings – Adjust background music and sound effects to your preference.
  • Ship Selection & Customization – Choose and configure your spaceship for exploration.
  • Leaderboard System – Compete with others and track your progress.
  • Mini-map Navigation – Stay oriented and explore the world more easily.
