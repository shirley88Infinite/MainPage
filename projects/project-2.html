Indie Game (Team Project): Blood Rising

Available on Steam

Visit the Steam Store Page to learn more or add to your wishlist.

Inspiration

During that time I was playing Hearthstone, I was inspired by its mix of deck-building strategy and fun RNG moments that kept every match fresh. I started wondering if I could create my own card game, and together with a designer friend we came up with a vampire theme infused with new elements.

Overview

This is a roguelike game with card battles as its core. Players need to play as a vampire ancestor to resist the attacks of a large number of adventurers and defeat three great heroes to achieve ultimate victory.

My Role: I worked with a teammate on the programming side, where my main responsibility was implementing the core combat system. In addition, I collaborated with the art team to experiment with AI-assisted asset design and generation, testing its potential for unique usability in the project.

Core Gameplay

  • Defeat enemies using your skills and convert them into servants to fight alongside you.
  • Servants can evolve into stronger units by defeating more enemies.
  • Enemies drop items and souls, which can be used to boost combat or upgrade skills.
  • Survive waves of heroes and adventurers—victory comes when all enemies are defeated.

Tech & Features

  • Implemented a simple turn-based system to manage player and enemy actions.
  • Designed a basic card system with drag-and-drop interactions and collision-based effects.
  • Built win/lose conditions and a minimal UI flow to support the core gameplay loop.
  • Added a lightweight multi-language system, allowing the game to be easily localized into different languages.

Walkthrough

Player's Turn – Attack

The player uses skills or cards to launch an attack against enemy units, starting the combat round.

Enemy Defeated – Loot Drop

When an enemy is defeated, it may randomly drop items or souls, which can be collected to strengthen the player's units or abilities.

Next Turn – Enemy Strikes Back

After the player's action, the turn passes to the enemy side, and the opponent launches its counterattack.
